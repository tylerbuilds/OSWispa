# Code of Conduct

MorpheOS Voice is an open-source project for people with different backgrounds, abilities and levels of technical experience. Project spaces should be useful, safe and welcoming.

## Expected conduct

- Be respectful and specific.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Assume good intent while still addressing harmful impact.
- Make room for people who communicate differently or need more context.
- Keep reports free of transcripts, recordings, credentials and other private data.
- Respect project boundaries, licences and the work of other projects.

## Unacceptable conduct

- Harassment, threats, discrimination or personal attacks.
- Sexualised language or unwanted attention.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Deliberately exposing transcripts, recordings, credentials or security details.
- Repeated disruption after a maintainer has set a clear boundary.
- Impersonating an official MorpheOS release or contributor.

## Reporting

For ordinary conduct concerns, email [hello@morpheos.net](mailto:hello@morpheos.net). For a security vulnerability, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) instead. Include only the detail needed to understand the issue.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, comments or accounts that breach this policy. They should respond proportionately, protect reporters' privacy and explain decisions where it is safe to do so.

## Scope

This policy applies in the repository, issue tracker, discussions and other spaces where someone is representing MorpheOS Voice or the project community.
