# Project name and identity

The MorpheOS Voice source code may be used, modified and redistributed under the [MIT licence](LICENSE).

“MorpheOS” and “MorpheOS Voice” identify the official project and releases published through [morpheos.net/voice](https://morpheos.net/voice). The MIT licence covers code; it does not give a fork permission to impersonate an official MorpheOS product.

If you publish a fork:

- use a different product name and visual identity unless MorpheOS has given permission;
- do not imply that MorpheOS endorses, supports or publishes your build;
- make changes and security responsibility clear to users; and
- keep the licence and copyright notices required by MIT.

Honest descriptions such as “based on MorpheOS Voice” are welcome when they make the relationship clear.

This is a practical project identity policy, not legal advice. Questions about official branding can be sent to [hello@morpheos.net](mailto:hello@morpheos.net).
